## Scientific Scope

The manuscript explores survivability as a **constrained property**, not as an optimization objective. It focuses on:

- Long-horizon system behavior
- Irreversibility and loss of recovery paths
- The limits of scalar representations for survivability
- Falsifiability through simplified toy models

The work is intentionally conservative in its claims. Negative or ambiguous results are treated as informative rather than disqualifying.

## Relationship to Simulations
